Today's recipe! - Bacon, Egg and Tacos

Every day we select a recipe to display on the front page to share with you some of the finests meals you can prepare at home on your own. Today's our front page recipe is "Bacon egg and Tacos", a breakfast rich with nutrients and with minimal preparation time. As the name implies, the recipe consists of preparing flour tortillas with bacon, scrambled eggs, and if desired some salsa as an addition. To prepare this breakfast recipe you'll need:

  • 6 eggs.
  • ¼ cup crumbled cooked bacon.
  • 2 tablespoons butter.
  • 3 slices of cheese cheese, diced.
  • 6 flour tortillas.
  • 1 cup salsa (Optional).

Quick-fix meal! - Simple Scones

For those who can't afford spending too much time preparing meals and food, we share recipes that are simple and fast to make. We call these quick-fix meals and this week we are sharing a simple yet great recipe for scones, which you can eat for breakfast, brunch or as dessert during other meals. For this recipe you'll need:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour.
  • ⅓ cup sugar.
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder.
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda.
  • ½ teaspoon salt.
  • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter, frozen.
  • ½ cup raisins (or dried currants)
  • ½ cup sour cream.
  • 1-2 eggs.

Traditional food! - Indian Tomato Chicken

A great cook is able to prepare foods of different flavours from the many corners of our world. That's why this week we are taking a look at recipe with traditional origins in India! The Indian tomato chicken is a meal rich with spices, herbs and taste, though one should regulate the ingridients if one isn't too tolerable to spicy food. These ingridients are:

  • 1 large onion, chopped.
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped.
  • 1 slice fresh ginger root.
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin.
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric.
  • 1 teaspoon salt.
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper.
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom.
  • 1 (1 inch) piece cinnamon stick.
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves.
  • 2 bay leaves.
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg.
  • 6 skinless chicken thighs.
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes, crushed.
